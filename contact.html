Skincare support frequently asked questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express delivery options are available at checkout for faster skincare product delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window on all unopened products. If you are unsatisfied with your order, contact us and our support team will help you with the return process.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all AURA skincare products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We focus on ethical, responsible clean beauty standards.

Can I use these products on sensitive skin?

Our formulas are dermatologist-tested and designed to be gentle on different skin types, including sensitive skin, but patch testing is always recommended.
